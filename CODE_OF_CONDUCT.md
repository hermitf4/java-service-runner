# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for all users and contributors.

## Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Providing constructive feedback.
- Being supportive toward new contributors.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate corrective action.
